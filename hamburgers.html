Hamburger Recipes

Description

Here is a recipe for the best burgers ever created

Ingridents

  • Hamburger Meat
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Ketchup
  • Hamburger Buns

Steps

  1. Cook the hamburger meat on stove top
  2. open the Buns
  3. place the hamburger patty on the buns
  4. place the desired amount of lettuce, tomato and Ketchup
  5. close the sandwich with the other patty
  6. enjoy!
