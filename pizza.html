Pizza Recipes

Description

Here you will learn how to make a delicious pepperoni pizza

Ingridents

  • pepperoni
  • Dough
  • mozzerella cheese
  • Tomato Sauce

Steps

  1. Kneed the dough into a circular shape
  2. Evenly disperse Tomato Sauce
  3. Shred and disperse the mozzerella cheese
  4. Place the pepperoni on top of the cheese
  5. Cook in the oven on 450F until golden brown on bottom
  6. Cut into triangular piece's
  7. Voila - Serve and enjoy!
