Cake Recipe

Description

You will learn how to cook a delicous cake

Ingridents

  • Cake Batter
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Chocolate icing

Steps

  1. Mix Cake batter with eggs
  2. put into a cake tray
  3. cook in oven at 350 degress
  4. remove and let cool for 30 minutes
  5. apply the chocolate icing
  6. cut into triangular pieces
  7. enjoy!
